# VIREKA Space

VIREKA Space is an interpretive support AI that helps distinguish what is observable from what may be assumed before decisions are made or prompts are written.

The system supports clearer understanding prior to response generation.

VIREKA does not provide advice.
It helps clarify interpretation so decisions and AI prompts begin from a more stable starting point.

Based on:

Beyond Thought: Awareness as Design Intelligence  
by Anthony Escobedo

---

## Core idea

Clarity before decision.  
Clarity before prompting.  
Structure before response.

VIREKA Space helps separate:

• what is directly observable  
• what may be interpreted  
• what remains uncertain  

When these distinctions are clearer, responses become more coherent.

---

## MVP scope

Web-based AI interface with two entry modes:

1. Clarify a situation
2. AI interaction

The system helps users:

• distinguish observation from interpretation  
• identify missing information  
• clarify what matters  
• improve AI prompt structure  

The system does not:

• provide therapy  
• provide coaching  
• prescribe decisions  
• simulate psychological authority
