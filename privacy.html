Privacy

Vireka Space is designed to support clarity by helping distinguish what is observable from what may be assumed.

Conversations may be processed in order to generate responses. No guarantee of confidentiality is implied.

No intentional collection of personal identifying information is performed beyond what is voluntarily entered into the interface.

Usage data may be used in aggregated form to improve system behavior and usability.

Users are encouraged not to include sensitive personal, medical, financial, or confidential information.

Vireka Space is an experimental environment connected to ongoing research into awareness and decision processes.
